문제
There are 
N
 people sitting in a circle, numbered clockwise from 
1
 to 
N
. 
They are playing a game of pass the towel. Initially, person number a is designated as 'it'. 
This person passes the towel clockwise 
K
 spaces. The person who receives the towel becomes 'it'. 
This process is repeated 
Q
 times. Write a program to determine the number of the person who is 'it' after the 
Q
 repetitions.


The first person to be 'it' is person number 
1
.


입력
On the first line, integers 
N
, 
K
, and 
Q
 are provided, separated by a space.
(
1
≤
K
<
N
≤
100
, 
1
≤
Q
≤
10
,
000
,
000
)


출력
Print the number of the person who becomes 'it' at the end.


예제 1 입력
7 1 10

예제 1 출력
4
