문제
Given an integer N, write a program to print the reversed N and the sum of its digits.
When reversing N, keep any leading zero in the result and print it as it is.

입력
The first line is given as an integer N.
(1≤N≤1018)

출력
Print the reversed N and the sum of its digits.

예제 1 입력
12345

예제 1 출력
54321 15
