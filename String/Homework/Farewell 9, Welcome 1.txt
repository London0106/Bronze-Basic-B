문제
Given a number less than 1,000,000, let's replace all the occurrences of 9 in each digit with 1 and output it!


입력
An integer less than 1,000,000 is given.


출력
Output the number after replacing all the occurrences of 9 in each digit with 1.
If there are no 9s in the number, output it as it is.


예제 1 입력
1995

예제 1 출력
1115

예제 2 입력
608

예제 2 출력
608
