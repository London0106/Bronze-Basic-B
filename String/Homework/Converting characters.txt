문제
Let's convert the uppercase letters to lowercase and the lowercase letters to uppercase in the given sentence and output it!


입력
A sentence without any spaces is given on a single line.
The length of the sentence is at most 20.


출력
Output the sentence with the cases of the letters swapped (uppercase letters become lowercase, and lowercase letters become uppercase).


예제 1 입력
HelloAlgo

예제 1 출력
hELLOaLGO

예제 2 입력
ASCIIcode

예제 2 출력
asciiCODE
