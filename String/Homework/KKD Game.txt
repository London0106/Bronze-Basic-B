문제
Kungkungdda(KKD) is a type of word chain game.


Given three words, let's determine if the KKD game can proceed normally or not.


입력
Three words are given separated by spaces.


출력
If the Kungkungdda game can proceed with the given three words, output "yes". Otherwise, output "no".


예제 1 입력
apple
elephant
triangle

예제 1 출력
yes

예제 2 입력
hey
hello
algo

예제 2 출력
no
