문제
Haru has to deliver 26 packages to 26 houses. 
Each house is assigned a number from ‘A’ to ‘Z’, and the destination of each package is also designated with a random character from ‘A’ to ‘Z’. 
When the packages delivered to each house from ‘A’ to ‘Z’ are given as a string, calculate the number of correctly delivered packages.

입력
From the first to the twenty-sixth line, the number of packages arrived at each house from 'A' to 'Z', and the information of the arrived packages are given, separated by a space. 
The number of arrived packages is greater than or equal to 0 and less than or equal to 100,000.

출력
Print the number of correctly arrived packages from 'A' house to 'Z' house, separated by a space.
