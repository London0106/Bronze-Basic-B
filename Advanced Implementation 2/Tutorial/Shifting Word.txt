문제
Given a word S consisting of lowercase English letters, Haru want to encrypt it. 


Encryption follows the following rule:
'a' becomes 'b', 'b' becomes 'c', 'c' becomes 'd', ..., 'y' becomes 'z', 'z' becomes 'a'.


Write a program to encrypt word S and print the resulting encrypted word.


입력
The first line contains the word S.
(1≤|S|≤100)

출력
Print the resulting encrypted word.


예제 1 입력
gdkkn

예제 1 출력
hello
