문제
Rainfall data is given for 
31
 days. 
Write a program that prints the day with the maximum rainfall and the amount of rain on that day.
If there are multiple days with the same maximum rainfall, print the information for the earliest day.


입력
On the first line, 
31
 integers are given, separated by spaces. Each integer is greater than or equal to 
0
 and less than or equal to 
100
.
These represent the rainfall amounts in order from the 
1
st day, 
2
nd day, ... up to the 
31
st day.


출력
Print the day with the maximum rainfall and the amount of rain on that day separated by space.
If there are multiple days with the same maximum rainfall, print the information for the earliest day.


예제 1 입력
4 6 4 2 1 5 4 1 2 3 8 2 5 8 3 1 10 6 1 9 9 7 4 10 8 1 1 7 2 1 2 

예제 1 출력
17 10
