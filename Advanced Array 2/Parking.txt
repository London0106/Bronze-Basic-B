A

B

C
 won per minute.

<

<

10
,
000
)

t
1
 represents the time when a car parked, and 
t
2
 represents the time when the car left the parking lot.
(
1
≤
t
1
<
t
2
≤
1
,
000
)


Note that 
t
1
 and 
t
2
 are inclusive of the parking time.


출력
Print answer.

10 20 30
1 5
2 5
3 6

130
